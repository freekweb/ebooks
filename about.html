ABOUT THE E-LEARNING MATERIAL

This E-learning writing material is based on the results of the study "Writing Performance of Senior High School Students and their Atitude towards Writing". It has been designed primarily to equip senior high school students with the fundamental writing skills they need to cope with the demands of language learning, specially enhancing their writing competence.

This lesson exemplar develops their competence in writing through the five components along content, organization, vocabulary, language use, and mechanics. Furthermore, it equips them with rules and conventions in writing that are critical and crucial in achieving a high writing performance..

The knowledge, skills and insights that the students will gain from this material may be used in various academic endeavors, chosen disciplines, and future careers specially that they will encounter numerous and challenging writing tasks and writing endeavors in various purposes and various fields.

LEARNING OUTCOMES

Language learners will be able to:
  • Discuss the importance of the nature of writing
  • Apply the different writing strategies in a writing activity
  • Utilize the different brainstorming strategies in creating an effective content for a writing task
  • Formulate writing content that are relevant and connected to the subject or topic of a writing task
  • Share insights about the importance of brainstorming strategies through a reflection
  • Apply the three main parts of writing an essay
  • Discuss the nature of organization and its importance in writing
  • Distinguish the distinction between cohesion and coherence
  • Utilize transitional devices in writing an essay
  • Formulate effective paragraphs with an appropriate topic sentence and supporting details
  • Apply the knowledge in diction and vocabulary in writing an essay
  • Formulate paragraphs with proper vocabulary
  • Develop mastery of the various rules in grammar and language use
  • Apply the rules and conventions in language grammar and structure
  • Utilize proper punctuation rules, capitaliation and spelling in writing an essay
  • Write an essay following the correct and proper conventions along content, organization, vocabulary, language use, and mechanics
